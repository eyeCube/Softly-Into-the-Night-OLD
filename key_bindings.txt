//In order to remove one binding, set it to: NONE 

//Bindings may begin with one or more special combination
//keys: Shift, Ctrl or Alt. Example key binding:
//Ctrl+a
//The action bound to this combination will only function
//if and when the a button is pressed while the Ctrl button
//is being held; if the Alt button is also being held, for
//instance, the input will be treated as different from Ctrl+a.
//Left and right Ctrl  are treated as the same;
//Left and right Alt   are treated as the same;
//Left and right Shift are treated as the same;

//NOTE: NumLock is currently unsupported. The numpad keys work
//only if you DO NOT have NumLock on. Keep it off during play.

//Bindings are not case-sensitive.
//to add shift, control, or alt to a key binding,
//prefix the key with:
//'Shift+', 'Ctrl+', or 'Alt+'
//example: Ctrl+Alt+DELETE
